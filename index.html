Sylhet Division: Known for its tea gardens, rolling hills, and abundant rainfall, Sylhet is a picturesque region with serene landscapes. The Sylheti countryside is dotted with lush fields, tea estates, and traditional villages

Countryside and Rivers: The rural areas of Bangladesh are characterized by vibrant green fields, tranquil rivers, and friendly villages. The countryside is dotted with ponds, canals, and wetlands that support diverse flora and fauna. Saint Martin's Island: Located in the Bay of Bengal, this small coral island offers clear blue waters, sandy beaches, and opportunities for snorkeling and diving

Hill Tracts: The Chittagong Hill Tracts offer stunning landscapes with lush green hills, rivers, and waterfalls. Places like Bandarban and Rangamati showcase the natural beauty of the hilly regions.
